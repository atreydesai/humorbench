7. **Timing**: Relates to the pacing or delivery of the joke, often used to build anticipation or create comedic effect.
8. **Meta-Humor**: Humor that comments on or refers to the joke itself, often self-referential.
9. **Punchline**: The final line or revelation that delivers the comedic impact of the joke.
10. **Redirection**: Shifts the focus away from the expected punchline or context.
11. **Non-Line**: A line that doesn't fit into any of the above categories, often filler or transition.
12. **Wrap-Up**: Concludes the joke or ties up any loose ends.
13. **Repetition**: Repeats information or a phrase for emphasis or comedic effect.
